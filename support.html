FAQ

Which areas does Witch Way cover?
Salem, Massachusetts.

What iOS versions are supported?
iOS 17 or later.

Why do you need my location?
Witch Way uses your location to show accurate distances and directions. Your location data stays on your device.

Does it work offline?
Yes. Recently viewed places and essential data are cached so you can keep going when service is spotty.

Is there an App Clip?
Yes — available at select landmarks (generic coverage).

How do I send feedback or suggest a place?
Email support@witchway.app with details. Screenshots help.

Troubleshooting

Location isn’t updating.
Open Settings → Privacy & Security → Location Services → Witch Way, and set to “While Using the App.” Ensure Low Power Mode or cellular restrictions aren’t blocking updates.

Universal links or App Clip aren’t opening.
Confirm you have a data connection. If using a password manager or private browsing, try opening the link in Safari first.

Privacy

We do not use third-party SDKs. We only use Apple’s built-in analytics tools to understand general usage. We don’t sell your data or build behavioral profiles.
